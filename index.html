Lindgreen Fencing are a locally owned and operated fencing company based in East Auckland.

We service the greater Auckland area and as far north as the Hibiscus Coast as well as the beautiful

Coromandel.  With over 15 years experience, we install all types of fencing.

 

From your villa picket fence to mesh and pipe security fencing, timber boundary fencing or driveway

gates we do it all…...but we also do more!

 

Garden box retaining to the large sub development retaining walls, small decks and large custom

designed decks with stairs and trellis screening and can’t forget the pool fencing for summer safety.

 

We work with some large construction companies providing fencing/landscaping to new housing.

The new Waimahia Inlet Housing Development in Weymouth and the new houses on Botany Rd opposite

A&E are something we are very proud of. We place as much emphasis and pride on the small private jobs as

customer satisfaction is our top priority.

 

Our main goal is to provide quality workmanship, great communication and an excellent end product

that you as the customer are 100% satisfied with.

 

We are happy to provide a free no obligation quote as well as any advice on ideas or designs of fencing

that would suit the property best.

Our Services include…

 

• TIMBER FENCING - Acoustic, Rail Over, Capped, Picket, Horizontal Panels, U Mould Panels,

   Trellis and Shiplap

 

• STEEL FENCING - Pipe and Mesh, Security, Powdercoated and Pool Fencing

 

• GATES - Pedestrian, Driveway, Swinging and Roller in Timber or Steel

 

• RETAINING WALLS - Garden Boxing, Base of Fences and Engineered Retaining Walls

 

• DECKS - Custom designed and built with Stairs, Built in Seating, Trellis screens and Boardwalks
